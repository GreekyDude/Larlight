Trump announces every fast-food restaurant in America is going to get banned

Thursday, 22nd May 2025 — By Micheal Nikey

In a stunning and surreal announcement, President Donald Trump declared that every fast-food restaurant in the United States will be banned — and will be replaced with a new chain he is launching, which will be called 'Trump Burger'.

Photo: Trump announcing the new only American fast food restaurant.

The announcement came during another Trump rally where he stated, “on our path to make America great again, I must also ban fast-food restaurants from the country, as they have become too European controlled. And we shall replace it with a new, purely american one, calld trump Burger"

Photo: Protest in Washington D.C. against fast foods ban

Since, many americans have expressed their disagreement by thousands of protests erupting in the country. Here is one of the biggest ones, happening in Washington D.C., with thousands of people, bearing signs like “save American culture” and “don’t let our history die”, along with flags with fast food logos.
